# Strategy Iteration Log

## S1 (Rejected)
- Full payment aggregation + all providers + ownership marketplace from day one.
- Rejected for compliance, cost, and execution risk under zero-resource constraints.

## S2 (Partially Accepted)
- Protocol-first + simulated settlement + adapter-based integration.
- Improved feasibility but weak incentive story for government and large enterprises.

## S3 (Adopted Baseline)
- Triple-value framing:
  - public value: transparency, anti-fraud, auditable operations
  - industry value: lower integration cost
  - business value: new distribution and monetization channels
- Two sandboxes + one pilot track:
  - payment sandbox
  - model-provider sandbox
  - local pilot

## S4 (Execution Policy)
- Standard first, platform second, ecosystem third.
- Revenue-share rights first, ownership transfer later.
- Receipt-driven accounting only; no direct fund custody.
- Auditability first; scale after robust traceability.
